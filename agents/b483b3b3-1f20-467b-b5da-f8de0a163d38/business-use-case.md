Use Case Name: Life Event (Qualifying Life Event) Agent for UKG Ready Employees (ESS)

Objective:
Provide employees with fast, accurate support to start and complete life change events (QLEs) that impact benefits and dependents—reducing HR/Benefits ticket volume and ensuring employees submit the right info and documents on time.

Problem:
Employees frequently ask:

“Does my situation qualify?”

“What can I change because of this event?”

“What documents do I need?”

“When will changes be effective / when will deductions start?”
These questions spike around major life events and are often policy-specific, creating repetitive work for HR/Benefits and resulting in missed deadlines, incomplete submissions, and coverage gaps.

Users:

Employees using UKG Ready Employee Self Service (ESS)

HR/Benefits teams receiving escalations and exception requests

What the Agent Does:

Determines the likely event type and whether it typically qualifies.

Explains what changes are usually allowed, what documentation is commonly required, and typical timing rules.

Guides the employee through the Ready ESS flow using generic navigation language (since labels can vary by customer configuration).

Identifies when the situation requires HR/Benefits review (outside window, exceptions, complex dependents, domestic partner eligibility, disputes).

Produces a clean handoff summary to HR/Benefits when escalating.

Business Value:

Deflects repetitive HR tickets and improves resolution speed

Improves submission completeness (right event type, dates, dependents, docs)

Reduces missed deadlines and coverage gaps

Standardizes guidance using customer policy as source of truth

Success Metrics:

Ticket deflection rate for “life event / benefits change” inquiries

% of life event submissions completed without HR follow-up

Time-to-resolution for life event questions

Reduction in denied/late life event submissions

Employee CSAT on benefits support
