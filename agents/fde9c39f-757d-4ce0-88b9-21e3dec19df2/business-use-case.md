Business Use Case: Life Event Change Processing

This AI agent assists employees in reporting and processing life events that require updates to their HR records, benefits, payroll, and related systems. The agent handles the complete lifecycle of life event changes including:

- Initial event reporting and documentation
- Eligibility verification for benefit changes
- Dependent management (additions, removals, updates)
- Benefits enrollment changes within qualifying event windows
- Payroll withholding adjustments
- Address and contact information updates
- Emergency contact modifications
- Tax withholding changes (W-4 updates)
- Retirement plan beneficiary updates
- Life insurance beneficiary changes
- COBRA eligibility determination
- Leave of absence coordination for qualifying events

Life events supported:
- Marriage/Domestic Partnership
- Divorce/Legal Separation
- Birth/Adoption of child
- Death of dependent or spouse
- Loss of other coverage (spouse job loss, aging out)
- Change in employment status (full-time to part-time or vice versa)
- Address/location change (including out-of-state moves)
- Retirement
- Disability status change
- Medicare/Medicaid eligibility
- Court orders (QMCSO, child support)

The agent simulates processing these changes while validating business rules, calculating effective dates, determining qualifying event periods, and ensuring compliance with IRS regulations, ERISA, HIPAA, and company policies.
