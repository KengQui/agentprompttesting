Business Use Case: Paycheck Q&A Agent (Employee Payroll Support)

This agent helps employees quickly understand their paycheck by answering common pay-related questions in plain language and guiding them to the right next step—without needing to open a ticket or wait for HR/Payroll support.

Problem it solves

Employees regularly have questions about pay amounts and paycheck details (e.g., “Why is my net pay lower?”, “What is this deduction?”, “When is my next pay date?”, “Where can I find my pay stub/W-2?”, “How do I update my direct deposit?”).

These questions create high ticket volume and repetitive work for HR/Payroll teams, especially around paydays, bonus cycles, and tax season.

Employees often don’t know where to look, what a line item means, or which policy/process applies, leading to frustration and delayed resolution.

Target users

All employees who receive paychecks (hourly, salaried, multi-state, leave/LOA scenarios)

HR/Payroll shared services and managers who field pay questions

What the agent does

Answers “how-to” and “explain this line item” questions (earnings, taxes, benefits, garnishments, retro pay, reimbursements).

Provides step-by-step guidance for common actions (download pay stub, update W-4, change direct deposit, view YTD totals).

Uses approved payroll policies and knowledge articles to give consistent answers and links employees to the correct self-service page.

Identifies when a question requires human help and routes the employee with the right context (category, required details, suggested ticket fields).

Business value

Faster employee support: instant answers 24/7, especially on paydays.

Reduced HR/Payroll workload: fewer repetitive tickets and shorter handle times.

More consistent and compliant messaging: answers grounded in approved policies and payroll definitions.

Better employee experience: less confusion and fewer escalations.

Success metrics

Ticket deflection rate for payroll inquiries

Reduction in average time-to-resolution for pay questions

Employee satisfaction (CSAT) for payroll support

Accuracy/compliance rate (answers match approved sources)

Percentage of interactions successfully resolved without escalation
